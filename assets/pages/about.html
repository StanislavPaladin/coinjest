What is CoinJets
Quality Services
Coin Income Quest founded on 21st September, 2018, is an AI Arbitrage investment company that is designed with great innovative AI and Blockchain technology to equip and empower people all over the world to help build their finances to a new turning point and to maximize their Quality of Life (QOL).
AI to the World
This phrase was made popular by the community in reference to Artificial Intelligence changing the world and now also reflects our optimism and excitement for this technology. No matter where you are in the world, when you look around you the world we live in is surrounded by Air and Water and those are one of the most important things that we as humans need to survive and live every day. We believe that AI has the same potential to change the world as it is already changing our financial system and the way we earn money.
Our Culture
Company culture is not just about bean bag, free lunch, or liking the same film. These things are nice, but they are not enough to make a good place at work. At Worldlinkcoin, we believe it’s about so much more than that. It’s a positive work environment that leaves you energized, not drained. It is being challenged to learn and grow every new day. It’s about right balance of flexibility and structure. It’s about knowing that you’re supported for the long term. It’s about the exhilaration of creating something new from scratch.
Our Mission
At Worldlinkcoin we are on a mission to spread to the world how with the use of our powerful and intuitive AI Platform we can help you earn passive income and create the right plan for your finances.
Our Values
It takes a special kind of geniuses and an advanced tool to equip and empower people all over the world to design and build their finances. We call it the New Future and we put it at the heart of everything we do.
One mission, one team
We leverage on collective genius in the journey of a unique mission and it will impact the world for generations to come. Our intelligence team is sourced from best university and institutions. This mission is bigger than all of us and the only way to achieve it is to work as a team. We deeply trust one another, communicate clearly and encourage growth by being open to feedback. We know that we are good at some things and that there are team members who are better than us at others. We are aware that every person in our team plays an integral part in the success of our mission.
