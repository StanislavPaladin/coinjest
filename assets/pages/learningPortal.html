How does Artificial Intelligence technology work?
The use of AI has completely revolutionized the field of trading. The technology allows you to process a huge amount of data, analyze their trading algorithms and make forecasts. It is important that the system is constantly self-learning, increasing the speed of data processing and increasing the efficiency of transactions. According to statistics, in the US, more than 80% of all transactions on arbitrage platforms are made by automated systems based on AI.
