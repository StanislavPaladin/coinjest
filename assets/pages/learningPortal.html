How does Artificial Intelligence technology work?
The use of AI has completely revolutionized the field of trading. The technology allows you to process a huge amount of data, analyze their trading algorithms and make forecasts. It is important that the system is constantly self-learning, increasing the speed of data processing and increasing the efficiency of transactions. According to statistics, in the US, more than 80% of all transactions on arbitrage platforms are made by automated systems based on AI.
What is the essence of Arbitrage Trading?
Trading on the digital currency exchange involves making a profit on the difference in the purchase and sale of the cryptocurrency exchange rate on various exchanges.
What does "cryptocurrency" mean?
Cryptocurrency is a kind of digital currency that is not backed by a material form (national currencies, gold, oil, etc.). The system is decentralized and self-regulating. The turnover of cryptocurrencies occurs automatically and is completely anonymous. Analysts predict the gradual replacement of national currencies by digital money, which will give people more opportunities and freedom.
What you need to know about Bitcoin?
It is the first digital payment system with the unit of circulation Bitcoin, created in 2009. Bitcoin exists outside the government, the banking system, and any intermediaries that could control transactions.
